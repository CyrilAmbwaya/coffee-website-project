about us

Why Choose our coffee?

The greatest quality arabica coffees in the world, known for their acidity, intensity, and depth of flavor, have historically been produced in Kenya. Our coffee is cultivated on the country's rich volcanic soil and has a special place among one of the finest cups around the globe.

The bright acidity, lovely sweetness, and a rich body, the winy finish marks the uniqueness of our coffee. Kenya produces some of the world's best Arabica coffees, and as a single-origin coffee, it receives appreciation at cupping tables.
